## About the project

Roksi Shop is a women's clothing online store. The goal: intuitive navigation so customers find what they want quickly and buy without friction.

## What we built

- Minimal catalog — focus on the product, not the interface
- Quick view — product details without leaving the catalog
- Streamlined checkout — minimum steps to purchase
- Responsive design for mobile shoppers

## Results

A clean design that keeps attention on the product and removes everything between browsing and buying.
