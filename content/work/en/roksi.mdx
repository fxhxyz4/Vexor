## About the project

Roksi Shop is a women's clothing online store. The goal was to design a website focused on fast and intuitive navigation through the catalog.

## Challenge

- Easy navigation across a large catalog
- Attractive product pages that drive purchases
- Simple and fast checkout flow
- Responsive design for mobile shoppers
- Feminine aesthetic combined with functionality

## Process

We analyzed competitors and identified key pain points — complex navigation, confusing checkout. Mapped user journeys from first visit to completed purchase.

Key decisions:

- **Minimal catalog** — focus on the product, not the interface
- **Quick view** — product details without leaving the catalog
- **Streamlined checkout** — minimum steps to purchase

## Results

A clean, modern design that highlights the product and simplifies the path from browsing to buying.
