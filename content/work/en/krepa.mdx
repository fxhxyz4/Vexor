## About the project

Krepa is a mobile workout app. We needed a landing page that captures the brand energy and converts visitors into downloads.

## What we built

- Animated hero with word-mask effect and phone mockup
- Features, Showcase, Testimonials, Pricing and FAQ sections
- Scroll animations via Framer Motion with `prefers-reduced-motion` support
- App Store and Google Play buttons with deeplinks
- Responsive from 320px — snap-scroll on mobile, grid on desktop

## Results

- Landing conversion: **6.1%** to download
- Lighthouse score: **98/100**
- Fully static — deploys to any host with no server required
