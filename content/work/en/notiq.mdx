## About the project

Notiq is a cross-platform note-taking app. The goal was to create a powerful yet simple tool for Desktop, iOS, Android and Web — fast, clean, and intuitive.

## Challenge

- Unified design across four platforms
- Instant idea capture with zero friction
- Structured content and powerful search
- Seamless sync between devices
- Minimal interface with no distractions

## Process

We started by defining core use cases — capturing ideas instantly, organizing information, accessing notes from anywhere. Special attention was given to reducing friction between thought and note.

Key decisions:

- **Minimalism** — only what's needed, nothing extra
- **Adaptive grid** — one design scales from mobile to desktop
- **Dark and light themes** — comfort at any time of day

## Results

A reliable, distraction-free app that adapts to different devices. Future steps include advanced organization tools, deeper search, and collaboration features.
