## About the project

Budzhetnyk is a personal Telegram bot for expense tracking. Type "Coffee 5" — expense recorded. No apps, no sign-ups, no forms.

## What it does

- Add expenses in one message — by text or just amount
- Automatically categorize based on keywords
- Show stats for day, week, month or year
- Generate charts directly in chat — pie and bar
- Export data to Excel
- Set monthly budget limit with warnings

## Results

The bot processes messages in **< 200ms** and deploys with a single Docker command. Supports Railway, Fly.io or self-hosted VPS.
