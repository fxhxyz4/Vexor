## About the project

Motiv is a contemporary clothing store offering stylish and modern apparel for fashion-forward individuals. The brand focuses on high-quality, trendsetting pieces that combine style, comfort and versatility.

## Challenge

- Design a logo that reflects the brand's modernity and style
- Create a brand identity for online and offline presence
- The logo must be minimal and scalable
- Convey a premium feel without pretentiousness

## Process

Started with market and competitor analysis in the contemporary fashion segment. Defined positioning — accessible premium for a young audience.

Developed several directions: from a minimal wordmark to geometric marks. The final solution — a clean wordmark with custom typography.

Key decisions:

- **Custom typography** — unique letterforms not confused with other brands
- **Monochrome** — logo works equally well on any background
- **Scalability** — from favicon to storefront signage

## Results

A clean, modern logo that captures the brand's character — style without unnecessary noise.
