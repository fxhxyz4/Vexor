## About the project

Motiv is a contemporary clothing store. They needed a logo: minimal, scalable, with a premium feel without pretentiousness.

## What we built

- Researched competitors in the contemporary fashion segment
- Explored several directions — geometric marks to wordmark
- Final solution: custom typography that stands apart from other brands
- Delivered in all formats — from favicon to storefront signage

## Results

A monochrome logo that works equally well on white and black backgrounds, on screen and in print.
